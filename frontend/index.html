Architecture Context

In Domain-Driven Design, Value Objects are immutable objects that represent concepts in your domain. Unlike Entities (which have unique identities), Value Objects are defined by their values.

Value Objects in Action

  • StockItemId - Unique identifier for inventory items
  • Quantity - Represents amounts of stock
  • StockItemName - Product names
  • TrackingNumber - Package tracking identifiers

Why This Matters:

  • Domain Integrity: Ensures critical business concepts cannot be accidentally corrupted
  • Business Rules: Makes invalid states (negative quantities, empty names) impossible
  • Event Sourcing: Safe to replay events and reconstruct domain state
  • Type Safety: Compile-time guarantees about data validity
